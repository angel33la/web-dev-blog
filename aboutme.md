### My Goal Here with Blogging

I’m a web development student learning in an accelerated online program. I write about the parts of learning to code that don’t fit neatly into timelines or course descriptions — falling behind, pushing through burnout, and staying in the program when quitting feels easier.

This site isn’t about shortcuts. It’s about honesty.

I am also using this as a way to document my journey and be able to have a way to vent and journal through this process. I am sure that I will end up writing about any and everything that comes to mind. So if you find anything I say offensive or just subjects that you do not enjoy or like, never has that been or will be my intention. In the end, this is for my own growth and development in web development and life.

I am so glad that you have found my page and I truly hope that my writing can possibly help someone going through these same struggles as I am facing. Feel free to reach out if you feel the need or if you are just simply wanting to say hello.
